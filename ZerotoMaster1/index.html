Simple Recipes for All

All the cakes on this website are easy to make and take very little time to prepare. Why not give one of the recipes a go!

Victoria Sandwich Cake

A delicious traditional recipe. Fill with jam and cream, or buttercream. The choice is yours!

Lemon Drizzle Cake

A refreshing treat to delight the tastebuds! It's difficult not to demolish this classic in just one sitting, so why not make two at once?

Flapjacks

They're easy to make and ready in half-an-hour. Add chocolate drops, desiccated coconut or any other fruit to suit your taste.

Ginger Cake

Try this moist and gooey stem ginger cake. You'll want to keep going back for more!

Scones

You can't get much simpler than this recipe. But don't let it's simplicity fool you - they're delicious!
