Ginger Cake Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 100g caster sugar
  • 125g butter
  • 1 egg
  • 225ml treacle
  • 300g plain flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons bicarbonate of soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cloves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 250ml hot water

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C / Gas Mark 4. Grease and flour a 23cm (9 in) square tin.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the sugar and butter. Beat in the egg, and mix in the treacle.
  3. In a bowl, sift together the flour, bicarbonate of soda, salt, cinnamon, ginger and cloves. Blend into the creamed mixture. Stir in the hot water. Pour into the prepared tin.
  4. Bake 1 hour in the preheated oven, until a knife inserted in the centre comes out clean. Allow to cool in tin before serving.
