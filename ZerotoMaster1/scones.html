Simple Fruit Scones Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 225g self-raising flour
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 50g cold butter, cut into pieces
  • 25g caster sugar
  • 50g sultanas
  • 120ml milk, plus a little extra for brushing

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 220 C / Gas Mark 7. Grease a baking tray.
  2. Measure out the flour and salt in a mixing bowl. Add the butter. Rub together with your fingertips until the mixture looks like breadcrumbs. Stir in the sugar and sultanas.
  3. Add the milk to the mixture and stir everything together. Work the mixture with your hands until you have formed a soft dough. Do not knead too much.
  4. Gently roll out the dough until it is 2cm thick (or about as thick as the height of your cutter). Cut out scones with round biscuit cutters dipped in flour. Put the scones on a baking tray, brush them with milk.
  5. Bake for 12 to 15 minutes. Leave them on a cooling rack for 5 minutes.
